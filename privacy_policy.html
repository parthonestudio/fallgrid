Introduction

FallGrid is a mobile puzzle game by ParthOne Studio. Scores and progress are stored only on your device. We do not require an account, and the current version does not send your gameplay data to our servers.

This Privacy Policy explains what information FallGrid uses, how we handle it, and your choices. By downloading or playing FallGrid, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

2

Who we are

FallGrid is published by ParthOne Studio.

3

What the app is

FallGrid is a mobile puzzle game for iOS and Android. It is intended for a general audience. After install, you can play offline. Scores and progress are stored on your device only; there is no global online leaderboard.

4

Information we collect

We do not collect personal information through FallGrid in the current version. There are no user accounts, registration, login, or social sign-in. The app does not transmit personal data to our servers unless noted below.

We do not collect your name, email, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone audio, or camera data.

Settings (such as sound levels and preferences) and game progress (such as scores, Data Shards, and upgrade purchases) are saved locally in app-private storage on your device (fallgrid_save.cfg). This data is not sent to ParthOne Studio in the current version.

If you enable haptics in Settings, the app may use short device vibration for feedback. This is not used to collect personal information.

On iOS, the app may use Apple required-reason APIs:

  • File timestamp — Used by the game engine for save files and asset caching (Apple required-reason APIs on iOS).
  • System boot time — Used by the game engine for timing and session logic (Apple required-reason APIs on iOS).
  • Disk space — Used to check available storage before writing save data (Apple required-reason APIs on iOS).
5

How we use information

In the current version of FallGrid:

  • Local save data is used only to remember your settings and progress on your device so you can continue playing.
  • Haptics, when enabled, are used only to provide vibration feedback during play.

We do not use your gameplay data for advertising, profiling, or analytics in the current build. We do not sell personal information.

6

Storage and retention

Save data and settings remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app. Uninstalling FallGrid removes local save data from your device, subject to how your operating system handles app removal.

Because we do not operate user accounts or cloud saves in the current version, we do not hold your game progress on our servers. There is no server-side account data for us to delete.

7

Children’s privacy

FallGrid does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app has no accounts, and game data stays on the device, which limits privacy risk. Parents and guardians should still supervise young children’s use of mobile apps and games.

If you are a parent or guardian and have questions about FallGrid, please contact ParthOne Studio (see Section 13).

8

Third parties

FallGrid is distributed through the Apple App Store and/or Google Play. Those stores have their own privacy policies for downloads and account data.

The current version does not include third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services. FallGrid does not use advertising or cross-app tracking and does not request camera, microphone, photo library, contacts, or location permissions unless listed in the store privacy label.

If we add features such as ads or analytics in a future version, we will update this Privacy Policy before they are released.

9

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of personal information, or to object to certain processing. Because FallGrid in its current version does not collect personal data on our servers and stores game data only on your device, most of your control is local:

  • Delete local data: uninstall the app, or clear app data through your device settings where available.
  • Reset tutorial: use Settings in the app.

For other privacy questions or requests, contact ParthOne Studio (see Section 13). We will respond to valid requests in line with applicable law where we are able to do so.

10

International users

FallGrid is available in various countries through the App Store and Google Play. If you use the app outside your home country, your device and the app stores may process information under their own rules and locations. ParthOne Studio does not receive your gameplay save file in the current version.

If you are in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or other regions with specific privacy laws, you may have additional rights. Contact ParthOne Studio (see Section 13) to exercise them where applicable.

11

Security

Local save data is stored in app-private storage on your device. Because we do not collect gameplay data on our servers in the current version, there is no ParthOne Studio database of your scores or progress to secure. No method of electronic storage is completely secure; we encourage you to keep your device updated and protected with a passcode or biometric lock.

12

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example if we add new features (such as ads or analytics) or if laws change. When we update it, we will post the new version on this page and change the effective date at the top. Continued use of FallGrid after an update means you accept the revised policy, unless applicable law requires otherwise.
