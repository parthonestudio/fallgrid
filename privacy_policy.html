Introduction

FallGrid is a mobile puzzle game by ParthOne Studio. Your game progress stays on your device and we do not require an account.

This Privacy Policy explains what information FallGrid and its third-party services may process, how it is used, and your choices. By downloading or playing FallGrid, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

2

Who we are

FallGrid is published by ParthOne Studio.

3

What the app is

FallGrid is a mobile puzzle game available on iOS and Android. You can play offline after installation. No account or registration is required.

4

Information we collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera.

Your game settings and progress are saved locally on your device and are not sent to ParthOne Studio.

When you play on a mobile device, the following categories of data may be processed by third-party services we use:

  • Device and app information — such as device model, operating system version, app version, and language. Used by Google services for ads, analytics, and crash reporting.
  • Advertising ID — a device-level identifier used by Google AdMob for ad delivery and measurement, where permitted by your device settings.
  • App usage events — general events such as session start or level completed, used to understand how the app performs. No personal account is required.
  • Crash and diagnostic data — error details collected when the app crashes, used to identify and fix bugs.
  • Approximate location — coarse location derived from your network connection by ad services. We do not request or use GPS location.

All of this data is handled by Google (AdMob, Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics) under Google's own privacy policy. ParthOne Studio does not receive or store this data on our servers.

5

How we use information

  • Game progress and settings — stored on your device so you can continue playing across sessions.
  • Ads — optional ads may appear during gameplay. Ad data is used by Google AdMob to serve and measure ads.
  • Analytics — app usage events help us understand how the game performs and where to improve it. Processed by Firebase Analytics.
  • Crash reports — crash data helps us find and fix technical issues. Processed by Firebase Crashlytics.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use data to build personal profiles or for direct marketing.

6

Storage and retention

Your game progress and settings are stored locally on your device. This data is removed when you uninstall the app or clear app data through your device settings.

ParthOne Studio does not maintain a server-side database of your game progress. Data processed by Google services is retained according to Google's privacy policy.

7

Children's privacy

FallGrid does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app requires no account and does not request personal details. Parents and guardians should supervise young children's use of mobile apps and games.

If you are a parent or guardian with questions about FallGrid, please contact us (see Section 13).

8

Third-party services

FallGrid is distributed through the Apple App Store and Google Play. Those platforms have their own privacy policies that apply to your account and purchases.

The following third-party services are active in this version:

  • Google AdMob — serves optional ads. Google Privacy Policy ↗
  • Firebase Analytics — collects anonymised usage events to help us improve the game. Google Privacy Policy ↗
  • Firebase Crashlytics — collects crash reports so we can fix bugs. Google Privacy Policy ↗

If we add new third-party services in a future version, we will update this policy before they go live.

9

Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information, or to limit how it is used. Because ParthOne Studio does not hold your personal data on our servers, most control is on your device:

  • Remove local data: uninstall the app, or clear app data in your device settings.
  • Limit ad personalisation: use your device privacy or advertising settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking; Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads).
  • Opt out of analytics: contact us and we will guide you on available options.

For any other privacy requests, contact ParthOne Studio (see Section 13). We will respond in line with applicable law.

10

International users

FallGrid is available worldwide through the App Store and Google Play. If you are in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, California, or another region with specific privacy laws, you may have additional rights. Please contact ParthOne Studio (see Section 13) to exercise them.

Data processed by Google services may be transferred internationally under Google's privacy policy and applicable data transfer mechanisms.

11

Security

Game data saved on your device is protected by your device's own security. We encourage you to keep your device updated and secured with a passcode or biometric lock. Data sent to Google services is encrypted in transit using industry-standard protocols.

12

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time — for example if we add new features or if laws change. When we do, we will post the revised version on this page and update the effective date. Continued use of FallGrid after an update means you accept the revised policy, unless applicable law requires otherwise.
