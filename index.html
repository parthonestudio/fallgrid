About the game

FallGrid is a mobile puzzle game by ParthOne Studio for iOS and Android. Merge equal tiles on a grid, score points, and survive rising difficulty — inspired by classic merge puzzles with quick, session-friendly runs.

Portrait One-thumb controls Offline play No account

Game modes

  • Campaign — Level-based goals. Same challenge for every player on each level.
  • Endless — Survive as long as you can. Difficulty ramps over time.
  • Daily Challenge — Same puzzle seed for everyone today. Complete all objectives!
  • Timed Chaos — 90 seconds — hit every objective before time runs out!

Features

  • Earn Data Shards and buy permanent upgrades in the in-app shop
  • Power-ups (CLR, ICE, BOM) and random modifiers keep each run fresh
  • Campaign, Endless, Daily challenge, and Timed chaos game modes
  • Local best scores on your device — no global online leaderboard
  • Optional ads in Campaign after Level 10; Data & privacy in Settings
  • Adjustable music, sound effects, and optional haptic feedback
  • Progress saved on your device — no cloud account required

Privacy-first. Scores and settings stay on your device. Read how we handle data in our privacy policy.
